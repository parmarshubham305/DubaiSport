Treadmill Details

The console on the ProForm 105 CST treadmill allows you to have all of your information and controls at your fingertips. To maximize your fitness sessions, choose your workout from the 6 preset weight loss workout programs with a single touch of the keys. The ProForm 105 CST, which is powered by a 1.75 CHP system, has more than enough power to withstand even the most strenuous cardio sessions. It's easy to find a comfortable running set up with a top speed of 16km/h and two positions adjustable incline (1.5–6%). You can run with wider strides in complete comfort with a walking belt that measures 41 x 127 cm. Furthermore, the impact is reduced with ProShox cushioning, allowing you to keep your balance.

You can easily view all of your workout stats thanks to the 5" high contrast multi-color display. The console displays information like time, distance, and calories burned, as well as Quick Speed controls for quick workout adjustments. With the integrated thumb pulse sensor, you can also keep track of your pulse and maintain your target heart rate. The 105 CST is equipped with iFit Bluetooth Smart (BLE), allowing you to get a new workout every day, track your progress automatically, replicate real-life runs with Google Maps, compete with friends, and tailor your training to your specific goals (iFit subscription not included). There are also six pre-programmed weight loss workout apps to work your way through!

The ProForm 105 CST Treadmill has a Space Saver design that allows you to store the machine vertically, and the EasyLift Assist delivers a powerful shock that helps lift the deck with a simple push.
